Starting Up Your Trezor Device

Introduction

Welcome to Trezor.io/Start! This guide will help you start up your Trezor device, ensuring your hardware wallet is securely set up and ready to use.

Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these steps to set up your Trezor device:

  1. Connect your Trezor device to your computer.
  2. Go to trezor.io/start to download the Trezor Bridge.
  3. Install the Trezor Bridge software.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

FAQ

What is a Trezor device?
A Trezor is a hardware wallet used for securely storing cryptocurrencies.
How do I update my Trezor firmware?
Connect your Trezor to the computer and visit trezor.io/start for firmware updates.
Can I recover my Trezor if it’s lost?
Yes, using your recovery seed, you can restore your wallet on a new device.
Is Trezor compatible with all cryptocurrencies?
Trezor supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies. Visit trezor.io/coins for a full list.
Where can I get support?
For support, visit the Trezor Support Center.
